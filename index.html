04 — About Me

I’m an computer engineer from NYU Shanghai who enjoys building hands-on, human-centered projects. My work combines software, hardware, and design, and I’m most interested in how technology can bring people together and improve everyday experiences. I like environments where ideas are tested quickly, iterated often, and shaped by user interaction.

I’ve worked on projects ranging from collaborative games with custom-built controllers to machine learning models for food safety and robotic systems that respond to people in natural ways. I’m drawn to making things that are interactive, and thoughtful — whether that’s a playful installation or a practical tool. At the core of everything I build is a desire to create work that is practical, technical, and a little bit fun.
