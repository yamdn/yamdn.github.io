The Challenge

Urban planning data is often fragmented across multiple government legacy systems. My goal was to create a unified pipeline that could scrape, clean, and visualize traffic patterns in real-time.

The Approach

I started by building a scraper using BeautifulSoup. The primary challenge was handling the unstructured CSV data coming from the municipal servers.

To ensure the data was accessible for non-technical stakeholders, I focused on high-contrast visualizations.

The Outcome

The final script reduced data processing time by 40%. This project is currently being used by the graduate research team at [University Name] to track rush hour congestion.
